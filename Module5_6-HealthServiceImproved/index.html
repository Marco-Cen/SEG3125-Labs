Our Services

Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy
($95/hour session)

Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy deals with the restoration of complete and unbarred function to the musculoskeletal system. It is also known as Orthopaedic Physiotherapy. The musculoskeletal system consists of one’s bones, muscles, tendons, ligaments, and joints.

Pelvic Floor Rehabilitation
($115/hour session)

This treatment provides specialized restorative intervention for women with pelvic dysfunction. Using a mixure of hands-on interventions and evidence-based practices, a customized assessment will be conducted. This sessions is ONLY available with our certified pelvic physician.

Neurological Physiotherapy
($125/hour session)

This treatment helps tackle any conditions related to the nervous system. From head injuries, stroke to dealing with chronic diseases such as Parkinson’s diseases, sciatica; this type of physiotherapy helps deal and cope with these disorders. As well, this helps regain ALL mobility, avoiding any diseases originating from the neurological or neuromuscular systems.

Cardiorespiratory/pulmonary/vascular Physiotherapy
($125/hour session)

Reccomended for individuals that suffer from diseases of conditions related to the heart, chest, and lungs. This treatment helps in the rehabilitation of individuals suffering from cardiac arrest or various chronic pulmonary disorders.
