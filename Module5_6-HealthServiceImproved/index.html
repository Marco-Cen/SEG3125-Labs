Services

Musculoskeletal Physiotherapy
($95/hour session)

Also known as the Orthopaedic Physiotherapy, this treatment deals with the restoration of complete and unbarred function to the musculoskeletal system: consists of bones, muscles, tendons, ligaments, and joints.

Rehabilitation and Pain Management
($100/hour session)

Customized treatments to remove pain in the body. Focuses on exercises that strengthen the body to reduce damages and increase recovery period for better mobility; helpful for post-surgery pain.

Select
Sports Physiotherapy
($110/hour session)

This treatment helps decrease the tension on the muscles of athletes and sports professionals; however, anyone can benefit from this treatment. These sessions are reccomended before and after each particular game for best results.

Select

Pelvic Floor Rehabilitation
($115/hour session)

This treatment provides specialized restorative intervention for women with pelvic dysfunction. Using a mixure of hands-on interventions and evidence-based practices, a customized assessment will be conducted. These sessions are ONLY available with our certified pelvic physician.

Cardiorespiratory, Pulmonary, Vascular Physiotherapy
($125/hour session)

Reccomended for individuals that suffer from diseases of conditions related to the heart, chest, and lungs, this treatment helps in the rehabilitation of individuals suffering from cardiac arrest or various chronic pulmonary disorders; this is a special ultimate bundle.
